About Us

Meet the team behind Daily Bread Ministries dedicated to sharing God's Word daily

Our Purpose

To glorify God by making His truth accessible and practical for everyday living, ensuring every believer has the spiritual nourishment they need.

Our Mission

To provide daily, biblically-sound devotionals that inspire, encourage, and challenge believers to deepen their walk with Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

1Daily Bread exists to strengthen believers to live in daily dependence on God through Jesus Christ—the true Bread of Life. We equip Christians with God’s Word so they are not spiritually starved, but built up in truth, grounded in faith, and ready for every battle of life. Our aim is simple and urgent: to feed God’s people with what endures, so they walk in obedience, courage, and steady trust—one day at a time.
